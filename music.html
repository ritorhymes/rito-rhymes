Altcoin Love

About the Song

"Altcoin Love" is a high-energy parody of Tupac and Dr. Dre’s iconic "California Love," voiced by Rito in a spot-on Dr. Dre impression. This catchy and fun track dives into the vibrant world of altcoins—cryptocurrencies outside of Bitcoin—blending cultural references, technical insights, and shoutouts to specific altcoins. With its infectious beat, clever lyrics, and top-tier production, "Altcoin Love" pays homage to the original while celebrating the diversity and innovation of crypto culture.

Encrypt Me

About the Song

"Encrypt Me" is a provocative and clever parody of Katy Perry's "E.T." featuring Kanye West, reimagined as a duet between a vulnerable database and a cunning ransomware virus. The song weaves a tale of temptation, control, and encryption through witty innuendos that blur the lines between romance and the mechanics of ransomware. With sharp wordplay and cultural nods, "Encrypt Me" turns a tech topic into an infotaining masterpiece of parody.

Blockchain 4 a Gangsta

About the Song

"Blockchain 4 a Gangsta" is a gritty parody of Master P’s "Is There a Heaven for a Gangsta", reimagined to explore underground crypto culture. Told from the perspective of a jaded OG crypto hacker who embraced blockchain before it went mainstream, the song captures his frustration with crypto’s transformation into a pop-culture phenomenon. Packed with raw emotion, sharp commentary, and vivid references to both underground and mainstream crypto culture, this track delivers a nostalgic yet biting critique of blockchain’s evolution.

Hodeler

About the Song

"Hodeler" puts a crypto twist on Kanye West’s iconic "Gold Digger," diving into the intriguing tale of a man navigating a relationship with a woman who seems to have a fortune in cryptocurrency. With a chorus that humorously proclaims, "Now I ain't saying she a hodeler, but she sure as hell ain't no broke coder," this parody delivers sharp wit and modern flair. Blending humor, cultural commentary, and a love for crypto, the song captures the essence of infotainment and creative storytelling.

Doge Doggy Coin

About the Song

"Doge Doggy Coin" is a smooth and clever parody of Snoop Dogg’s classic "What’s My Name?" reimagined as an anthem for Dogecoin. Featuring Rito’s sharp, witty verses and Eline Vera’s standout vocals on the catchy chorus, the song celebrates the fun and community-driven spirit of Dogecoin. With references to Dogecoin’s rise, its playful Shiba Inu mascot, and its role in the crypto world, "Doge Doggy Coin" delivers a perfect mix of humor, creativity, and charm.

A Trillie

About the Song

"A Trillie" is a high-energy parody of Lil Wayne’s iconic "A Milli," reimagined for the extravagant world of crypto whales. This punchline-packed track blends rapid-fire lyrics, hilarious crypto references, and a tribute to the eccentricity of crypto culture. From Bitcoin and Satoshi Nakamoto to celebrity crypto enthusiasts, "A Trillie" mirrors Lil Wayne’s random, unpredictable style while celebrating the swagger and absurdity of extreme crypto wealth.

One More Meme

About the Song

"One More Meme" aka Doge Meme Anthem is a vibrant and uplifting parody of Daft Punk’s iconic "One More Time", celebrating the joyful spirit of Doge memes and the positivity they inspire. With catchy lyrics and an infectious beat, the song encapsulates the essence of meme culture, spreading good vibes and a sense of community. Perfect for Dogecoin enthusiasts and anyone who loves a fun, creative twist on a classic track.
