Who is Rito Rhymes?

By day, Rito is a Chief Integrations Officer across Product, Brand, UX & Technology through his consultancy and studio RitoVision, but by night... he is an infotaining rapper and wordsmith blending clever rhymes, satire and thought-provoking performances that entertain and inspire. Whether delivering biting satire, insightful knowledge or heartfelt lyrics, Rito’s artistry pushes the boundaries of how music and lyricism can be used to achieve unconventional purposes, combining the power of words with his broader purview of cross-domain mastery in the world of technology.

How has he channeled his lyrical musical powers in ways that stand out?

-Performing lyrical presentations in a business-context for authoritative organizations in the blockchain industry

-Impromptu rapping of answers to interview questions on live radio

-Taking the stage with a Grammy Winner to energize the crowd (and breaking a mic stand in the process)

-Producing a whole catalogue of anthemic infotaining songs about technology parodying classic hit tracks (Check out the music page for that)

-Creating the first "rap NFT" on the Dogecoin blockchain

No one can be everything to everyone, but Rito Rhymes takes the most of Rito's creative passions and drives it in a way to share as much as he can. He is a lyrical liaison of visionary nerdness, so proceed with caution if you weren't looking for inspiration... because it just might strike anyway.
