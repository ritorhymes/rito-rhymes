A Snapshot of Rito in the Wild

Live Radio Interview

Rito made a memorable appearance on The Other Side of Midnight with Frank Morano on WABC77, New York's flagship radio station. During the episode, titled "Insane in the Blockchain," Rito rapped impromptu in response to mid-conversation and technical questions, seamlessly blending humor and insight while addressing topics ranging from crypto and the metaverse to mental health.

WABC77

Magazine Feature

Rito Rhymes, known for blending humor, insight, and rhyme, was featured in Stockhead for an interview titled, "Bitcoin is the Gangsta Rap of the Tech World: Putting the Verse in Metaverse with Crypto Rapper Rito Rhymes." During the interview, Rito delivered spontaneous crypto-themed rhymes while discussing topics like Bitcoin, Dogecoin, NFTs, and the metaverse, showcasing his unique ability to infuse technical discussions with creative expression.

Stockhead

Viral Content Creation

Rito's innovative content was featured by Screen Rant for creatively addressing a longstanding Call of Duty: Warzone bug that causes Riot Shields to disappear, frustrating many players. By combining spoken-word rap with gameplay footage, Rito Rhymes brought significant attention to this issue, helping players navigate the glitch until a fix was implemented. This video and story was among a broader campaign where Rito's video content and musical abilities aided the community in navigating bugs and glitches found in the blockbuster game.

ScreenRant

Performance Coverage

Dogepalooza, a Dogecoin-themed music festival, featured a lineup of indie and Grammy-winning artists. Rito delivered an insightful and engaging acapella explaining the Dogecoin Foundation’s roadmap which was was later shared and acknowledged by the Foundation and U.today, highlighting his unique ability to translate complex concepts into digestible format. Rito also performed alongside Grammy winner Damon Elliott in a classic set! Rito chronicled his experience at the festival in Hackernoon and was nominated for several contributor awards.

Hackernoon U.Today

Short Bio

Rito Rhymes is the lyrical, infotaining and oftentimes satirical persona of Rito’s multi-faceted creativity, where words take center stage as tools for storytelling, entertainment, and sharp social commentary. As an infotaining rapper and wordsmith, Rito blends clever rhymes, thought-provoking themes, and dynamic performances to craft narratives that both entertain and inspire. Whether delivering biting satire, insightful knowledge or heartfelt lyrics, Rito’s artistry pushes the boundaries of what’s possible in modern rap, combining the power of words with his broader expertise as a multimedia artist.

Rito Rhymes isn’t just about music—it’s a platform for creative expression that bridges humor, poetry, and cultural critique, offering a fresh perspective on the world. With a talent for turning everyday moments into thought-provoking verse, Rito’s work exemplifies the fusion of intellect, emotion, and technical mastery. This persona complements his broader artistic journey, presenting a side of Rito that thrives on rhythm, storytelling, and the transformative power of satire.
